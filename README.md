# BMW Sales & Market Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing BMW sales performance across multiple car series.

## Key Insights

* Sales trends by year
* Revenue analysis
* Regional sales distribution
* BEV (Battery Electric Vehicle) share trends
* Comparison across BMW model series

## Dashboard Sections

### BMW 3 Series

Analysis of units sold, revenue trends, and regional sales distribution.

### BMW 5 Series

Sales performance and BEV adoption trends for BMW 5 Series.

### BMW MINI

Insights into MINI model sales trends and revenue performance.

### BMW iX & i4

Electric vehicle sales analysis and BEV market trends.

### BMW X Series (X3, X5, X7)

SUV sales performance and regional market distribution.
